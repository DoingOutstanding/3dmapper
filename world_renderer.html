3D World Renderer

Loads the parsed map JSON files and renders rooms as spheres with their exits as link lines. Use left-click to rotate, right-click to pan, and wheel to zoom.

The global origin (0, 0, 0) is anchored to "The Grand City of Aylor" room with id 2 at map coordinates (11, 12).

  • Cyan spheres: area entrances.
  • Purple spheres: rooms with PK enabled.
  • Orange lines: two-way exits.
  • Pink lines: one-way exits.
Select a room to see details.
